Biography

I am a data scientist and computational psychologist with 6+ years of experience developing bespoke probabilistic models of risky decision-making, counterfactual reasoning, reinforcement learning, and latent variable measurement. I use principles from machine learning, decision theory, causal inference, and psychometrics throughout my work. Altogether, this framework allows for me to extract reliable and meaningful signals from noisy observational data, enabling me to make the right data-driven decisions given imperfect or incomplete information.
